About Me

I'm a senior undergraduate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Madras. Currently, I'm working on my bachelor's thesis titled "Heat Transfer Studies in Rotating Heat Sinks", under Prof. Chakravarthy Balaji. Additionally, I'm working under Prof. Ratna Kumar Annabattula on granular systems and under Prof. Manu Jaiswal on phase-field modelling. I was fortunate enough to have spent the summer of 2016 at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, where I was introduced to phase field modelling by Prof. Britta Nestler. My areas of interest include (1) Heat transfer and fluid mechanics, particularly in granular systems, (2) Physics of granular media and (3) Phase-field modelling. My research interests are quite diverse, as I genuinely enjoy solving new problems and exploring unfamiliar territory. I hope to pursue an MS in Mechanical Engineering in the near future.

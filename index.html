About Me

Hi! I'm a PhD student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University, working under the guidance of Prof. Arun Majumdar. My areas of interest are quite diverse, as I genuinely enjoy solving new and difficult problems. A lot of my work spans energy transfer and fluid dynamics, and my current focus is on heat transfer at the nanoscale. Ze Zhang, Prof. Majumdar, and I are trying to demonstrate phonon localization in certain materials. Our long term goal is to be able to create the perfect thermal insulator.

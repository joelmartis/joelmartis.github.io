About Me

I'm a senior undergraduate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. Currently, I'm working on my bachelor thesis titled "Heat Transfer and Optimization Studies in Rotating Heat Sinks", under Prof. Chakravarthy Balaji. Additionally, I'm working under Prof. Ratna Kumar Annabattula on granular systems and under Prof. Manu Jaiswal on phase-field modelling of dendritic growth. I was fortunate enough to have spent the summer of 2016 at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, where I worked on phase-field modelling under Prof. Britta Nestler. The areas that I'm interested in include:

  • Heat Transfer and Fluid Mechanics
  • Mechanics of Granular Media
  • Phase-Field Modeling

My research interests are quite diverse, as I genuinely enjoy solving new problems and exploring unfamiliar territory. I hope to pursue graduate studies in the near future.

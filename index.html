About

I am a senior year undergraduate student in the Mechanical Engineering Department at IIT Madras. Currently, I'm working with Prof. Chakravarthy Balaji, Prof. Ratna Annabattula and Prof. Manu Jaiswal on problems in computational heat transfer, heat transfer in granular systems and phase field modelling respectively. I spent the summer of 2016 at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, working under Prof. Britta Nestler, where I was introduced to phase field modelling and its diverse applications. My interests lie in heat transfer and fluid mechanics, especially in granular systems, and in phase field modelling. I wish to pursue a master's and a PhD in these areas.
